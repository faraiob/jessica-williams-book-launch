About Jessica Williams

The work and art of ceramic making has been a part of my family life for generations. Born in Namibia, pottery played a vital role in our daily lives. In the early 2000s, I decided to fully dedicate my career to this artistry after several years working in project management for a creative agency. My work in ceramics for me is a source of calm and focus that bears similarities to meditation. My process begins with research for each piece before I begin to make it, inspired by nature and form. It has been an honor showcasing these pieces in Namibia and around the world including Istanbul, Cairo, Peru, New York, to name a few. My finished works gravitate towards simplicity and bold presentations, and I hope this book is a visual enjoyment for your leisure. Heartfelt thanks to my friends and associates at the studio in London as well as Search Press UK and Ceramics Southern Africa for their support.
